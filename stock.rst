Stock

.. glossary::

    `Inventory Location </projects/trytond-stock-inventory-location/en/5.0>`_

    `Split </projects/trytond-stock-split/en/5.0>`_

    `Supply </projects/trytond-stock-supply/en/5.0>`_
        Supplies warehouses.

    `Supply Day </projects/trytond-stock-supply-day/en/5.0>`_
        Compute supply date per week day.

    `Supply Production </projects/trytond-stock-supply-production/en/5.0>`_
        Supplies warehouses with production orders.
